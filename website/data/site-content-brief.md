- **Experience number inconsistent** (27 vs 30+ years) — pick one.

- **Dated product-spec copy** (SR-260, EA-5 model numbers) — sell outcomes and lifestyle, not SKUs; move specs to secondary detail.
- **No builder/architect/interior-designer trade page** — new-construction pre-wire is implied by team experience but never pitched.
